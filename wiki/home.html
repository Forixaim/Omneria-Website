Omneria Archives

The Omneria Official Wiki

The Omneria Archives are one of the most ancient libraries in Omneria, giving away vital information for new travelers and more. There are only a few who view the Archives and therefore are often lost to myth and legend. People may be wondering what are the archives? What do they mostly comprise of? The truth is... We don't know. But, in a few moments you can recognize and hope to understand everything, learn everything, and best of all... master everything. There is more than pages and books that are to be learned from here there are one of the most coveted files of characters, common creatures, and even adversaries that you have fought. There are even those who you fought to gain their trust, to gain their acceptance. That is the true nature of the Archives. What are you now seeing is something that is usually available to everyone, but nobody has ever gotten the chance to look, there are historical records of Omneria, kept clean and virtually free from ink decomposition as well as digital records that has been kept refreshed automatically by the S.A.V.E. system, a complex set of machines that are only accessible by you and you only. Their programmed from hardware to only perform the following. Scan: to scan the setting for potential failing records. Archive: to store new information into the expanding library. reVitalize: To refresh and transfer old archive storage to the newest technology, which is currently being performed. Evaulate: To check the finished reVitalized information to see if it is ready for viewing. All these functions keep the archive as clean and ready to go as quickly as possible.
